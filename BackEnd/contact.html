Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line directly.

Can I visit your office for in-person assistance?

Yes, you're welcome to visit our office during our working hours. We recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure the relevant team member is available to assist you.

Do you offer technical support for course-related issues?

Yes, we provide technical support for all course-related issues. You can reach our technical support team via email at support@academicaffiliation.com or through the contact form on this page.

How can universities partner with Academic Affiliation?

We welcome partnerships with educational institutions. Please select "Partnership Opportunities" in the contact form subject line, and our partnership team will reach out to discuss collaboration options.
